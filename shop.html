Return Policy
Last updated: March 2025

30-day returns. We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for a full refund to your original payment method. Items must be unused and in original packaging.

How to Return an Item

Initiating a return is simple. Follow these steps:

  • Log in to your account and navigate to My Orders
  • Select the order containing the item(s) you wish to return
  • Click "Request Return" and select your reason
  • Print the prepaid shipping label emailed to you
  • Drop your parcel at any carrier location within 7 days

Return Eligibility

Items are eligible for return if they are:

  • Returned within 30 days of the delivery date
  • Unused and in original condition
  • In original packaging with all tags attached
  • Not marked as "Final Sale" at the time of purchase

Refund Timeline

Once we receive and inspect your return (typically 2–3 business days), your refund will be processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method. You'll receive an email confirmation when the refund is issued.

Exchanges

We do not process direct exchanges. If you'd like a different item, please return the original purchase for a refund and place a new order. This ensures the fastest processing time.

Damaged or Incorrect Items

If you receive a damaged, defective, or incorrect item, please contact us within 7 days of delivery at [email protected] with your order number and photos. We'll arrange a free return and replacement or full refund at no cost to you.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Items marked "Final Sale"
  • Gift cards
  • Personalized or custom-made items
  • Items returned more than 30 days after delivery
Our Story

Made with intention

ARCA was founded in 2019 with a simple belief: the objects in your home should be as thoughtfully made as the life you're building. We work exclusively with independent makers and small-batch producers who share our commitment to quality and sustainability.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 3–7 business days. Expedited (2-day) shipping is available at checkout.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to Canada, the UK, Australia, and select EU countries. International orders typically arrive in 7–14 business days.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of placement. After that, they enter fulfillment and cannot be changed. Please contact us immediately at [email protected].

How do I return an item?
