Return Policy
Last updated: March 2025

30-day returns. We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. Items must be unused and in original packaging.

How to Return an Item

Follow these steps:

  • Log in to your account and go to My Orders
  • Select the order and click "Request Return"
  • Print the prepaid shipping label emailed to you
  • Drop your parcel at any carrier location within 7 days

Return Eligibility

  • Returned within 30 days of delivery
  • Unused and in original condition with all tags
  • Not marked "Final Sale" at time of purchase

Refund Timeline

Once received and inspected (2-3 business days), refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to your original payment method.

Exchanges

We do not process direct exchanges. Return the original item for a refund and place a new order.

Damaged or Incorrect Items

Contact us within 7 days at hello@arca-living.com with your order number and photos. We will arrange a free return and replacement.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Items marked "Final Sale"
  • Gift cards
  • Personalized or custom-made items
Our Story

Made with intention

ARCA was founded in 2019 with a simple belief: the objects in your home should be as thoughtfully made as the life you are building. We work with independent makers who share our commitment to quality and sustainability.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 3-7 business days. Expedited 2-day shipping is available at checkout.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to Canada, the UK, Australia, and select EU countries. International orders arrive in 7-14 business days.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of placement. Contact us at hello@arca-living.com.
